Meet Daphne

The Woman Behind the Rebellion

I'm Daphne James — 59 years old, bold as ever, and completely done with the idea that fashion has an expiration date. I created Closet Rebel for every woman who's been told to "dress her age" and thought — absolutely not.

I believe in dopamine dressing. In wearing the bold print, the statement necklace, the kimono. In building a wardrobe that makes you feel like yourself — the most vibrant, alive version of yourself.
